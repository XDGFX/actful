Hi!


We're Callum and Georgie!




We decided to make this website after discovering a more sustainable alternative to buying shampoo in a plastic bottle - shampoo in solid bar form like soap!



We both wanted to be more sustainable - but we wanted an easy way of finding more sustainable alternatives to products we already used.



After some research we realised there was a gap in the market... so here we are!



How?



We've spent lots of time researching the best brands to use if you're looking for sustainably, plus we already had a load brands we already use and love to recommend!



We try to highlight brands we like for a number of reasons; primarily sustainability but also charitable values, better user experience, improved consumer privacy and much more can lead into the selection process!
